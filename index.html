I was born in Craiova, the 6th largest city of Romania and the capital of Dolj County. Craiova is situated in the south-west of Romania, 225 km west from the capital Bucharest.
My birth date (3rd of October, 1984) was a special day for the people of Craiova and also of Romania: the local football team CS Universitatea Craiova won 1-0 and 5-3 after the penalties from the spanish force Real Betis! Craiova football team qualified for the second round of UEFA CUP (season 1984-1985). So yes, I like football and I am a fan of Universitatea Craiova football team.
